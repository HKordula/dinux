How DINUX Park Works

DINUX Park is the world's first real dinosaur park, blending cutting-edge science with unforgettable adventure. Our team of paleogeneticists has brought dinosaurs back to life using advanced DNA reconstruction, allowing visitors to experience these magnificent creatures up close.

  • Guided Tours: Explore the park with expert guides and learn about each dinosaur species in their naturalistic habitats.
  • Interactive Exhibits: Touch real fossils, watch live feedings, and participate in hands-on science labs.
  • Safety First: Our enclosures are designed with the latest technology to ensure both visitor and dinosaur safety at all times.
  • Community Forum: Join our online forum to discuss discoveries, share photos, and connect with other dino-enthusiasts!

Whether you're a scientist, a student, or just a fan of dinosaurs, DINUX Park offers a once-in-a-lifetime adventure!
